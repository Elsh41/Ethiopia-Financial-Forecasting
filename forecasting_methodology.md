# Task 4: Forecasting Access & Usage (2025–2027) Methodology & Interpretation

## 1. Projected Outcomes Summary (Base Case)
* **Account Ownership (Access):** Projected to grow from **49.0% (2024)** to **52.4% (2025)**, **55.6% (2026)**, and **58.8% (2027)** under the Event-Augmented Base Case.
  * **National Target Assessment:** Under the Optimistic scenario, Ethiopia reaches the **60.0% National Financial Inclusion Strategy (NFIS)** target by late **2026 / early 2027**. Under the Base Case, it crosses the 60% mark in early **2028**.
* **Digital Payment Usage:** Projected to expand rapidly from **28.5% (2024)** to **33.2% (2025)**, **38.4% (2026)**, and **43.8% (2027)**.

---

## 2. Model Structure & Scenario Framework
1. **Trend Baseline:** Log-linear extrapolation of historical Findex cycles (2011–2024).
2. **Event-Augmented Base Case:** Integrates lagged cumulative shocks from Safaricom M-Pesa merchant acquisition, agent network expansion, and NBE interoperability mandates (+1.2 pp to +3.8 pp boost).
3. **Optimistic Scenario:** Models rapid smartphone price drop, foreign fintech entry, and full P2P interoperability (+4.5 pp boost by 2027).
4. **Pessimistic Scenario:** Models ongoing FX constraints, agent liquidity crunches, and slow 4G rural expansion (-3.0 pp drag by 2027).

---

## 3. Key Events with Largest Projected Impact
1. **Safaricom M-Pesa Merchant Payment Scaling:** Represents the primary growth driver for **Usage**, moving customers from peer-to-peer transfers to retail ecosystem transactions.
2. **NBE Open Banking & Interoperability Directives:** Enables seamless bank-to-wallet transfers, significantly reducing friction between formal bank accounts and mobile wallets.
3. **Government-to-Person (G2P) Digitization:** Shifting social safety net payments directly to digital accounts provides a structural floor for female account adoption.

---

## 4. Key Uncertainties
* **Agent Network Liquidity:** Cash-in/cash-out (CICO) agent coverage outside primary urban centers.
* **Macroeconomic Conditions:** Foreign currency shortages impacting telecommunications hardware and network expansion.
